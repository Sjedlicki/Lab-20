Hi there, my name is Steve. I don't like blogging or social media. I don't even know how to blog.

My journey to becoming a developer started back in October of 2018. I was playing Path of Exiles with friend and in the game we used a script that was created to filter and highlight valuable items within the game. I was suggesting to my friend that it would be nice if I could understand how this script is built, so that I might make my own edits to it. He told me that his roommate went to Grand Circus coding school and he absolutely loved it and recommended it to anyone interested in coding. Fast forward a few months, and here I am, creating this website while you reading this.
